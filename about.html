Project Purpose

Starspace is an educational initiative designed to introduce students to cutting-edge technologies in computer science. Our mission is to:

  • Demystify complex technical concepts
  • Showcase career opportunities in emerging fields
  • Inspire the next generation of technologists
  • Provide accessible learning resources


Educational Context

This project was developed for my MYP5 Comp Sci project. It serves as both an educational resource and career guidance tool, highlighting three key technology areas:

Large Language Models

Exploring the AI revolution in natural language processing

Quantum Computing

Understanding the next frontier in computational power

Robotics

Discovering the intersection of hardware and AI

Technical Details

      JavaScript for interactive elements
      Responsive design for all device sizes

      Github pages for easy hosting
